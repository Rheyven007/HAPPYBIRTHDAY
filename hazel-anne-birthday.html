For Hazel Anne
Dear Hazel Anne,

Happy Birthday, Lovey ❤️

There are many ways to greet someone on their birthday, but none of them feel enough for someone who became such an important part of my life. Today, I don’t only celebrate your birthday — I celebrate the person who changed me, understood me, stayed with me, and loved me through every version of myself.

For the past 3 years and 1 month, you’ve been more than just my girlfriend. You became my comfort, my peace, and the person I always want beside me no matter how hard life gets. I still remember how your smile first caught my attention, but it was your heart, your understanding, and the way you carry yourself that made me fall deeply in love with you.

You have this way of making everything feel lighter whenever I’m struggling. Every time I feel tired, lost, or pressured by life, you remind me that I’m capable and that I can keep going. Those moments may seem simple, but they mean everything to me because they came from you. Your love gives me strength in ways I cannot fully explain.

When I think about us, I think about all the moments we shared together our beach dates, our quiet home dates, our laughter, our private moments, and even the crazy memories that only we would truly understand. They may look ordinary to others, but to me, those moments became pieces of a life I never want to forget.

Our relationship was never perfect, and I think that’s what made it real. We went through misunderstandings, pain, mistakes, and even a breakup that almost ended everything between us. But despite all of that, we still found our way back to each other. That alone tells me how genuine our connection is. Thank you for giving me another chance. Thank you for choosing to stay even after seeing my flaws and imperfections.

I know there were moments when I hurt you, disappointed you, or became difficult to understand, and for those things, I am truly sorry.

One of the things I admire most about you is your heart — the way you love, the way you care, and even the smallest gestures you do without realizing how much they affect me. Something as simple as your kisses, your hugs, or your presence already brings me comfort. You make me feel loved in ways words cannot fully express.

And honestly, when I think about my future, you are always part of it. I don’t just see you as someone I love right now — I see you as the woman I want to build a life with, the future mother of my children, and the partner I want beside me through every stage of life.

So on your birthday, I hope you truly realize how valuable and deeply loved you are. I hope life gives back all the kindness and love that you continuously give to the people around you. You deserve happiness, peace, success, and all the beautiful things this world can offer.

Thank you for loving me, understanding me, and growing with me through the years. No matter where life takes us, a part of me will always be grateful that I met someone like you.

Happy Birthday, Lovey. I love you more deeply than words can explain ❤️

With so much love 💕 🌸 💖 ✨
